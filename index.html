How It Works

Weekly report — a consolidated list of your department's absentees goes to Mr. Sarris and Ms. Kyskira by email every Friday by 17:00, with names, exact dates of absence, and the reason.
Weekend duty — allowed only on Saturdays, Sundays and public holidays. "Swapped with" records who actually covered the shift if it was exchanged (shown as → in the Planner).
Vessel attendance — always requires the vessel name.
Public holidays — calculated automatically for any year, including Orthodox Easter. Extra company dates can be added in Settings.
Counting — absence days are counted on working days only; weekends and public holidays never count as leave. Vessel attendance and weekend duty are WORKING time, counted on any day including weekends.
Duplicates — duplicate employees, vessels and overlapping entries are flagged automatically before saving.
Data — entries are saved in this browser only. Export a backup (Settings → Data) at least weekly.
